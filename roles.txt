ROLES AND THEIR FUNCTIONS

1. Admin
	- Create laboratory groups and assign a lab head to each group. Only faculty members can be lab heads.
	- Change a laboratory's assigned lab head.
	- Confirm/activate faculty members that sign up.
	- View all labs, users, and experiments.
	- Activate/Deactivate user accounts.

2. Lab Head (must have faculty account)
	- Add/confirm faculty and graduate users to its assigned lab.
	- View all lab users and their experiments.

3. Faculty
	- Create experiment.
	- Publish own experiment any time.
	- Approve publication of graduate's experiments, given that they belong to the same lab.

4. Graduate/Student
	- Create experiment.
	- Must ask permission from faculty member of the same lab before publishing experiment.
